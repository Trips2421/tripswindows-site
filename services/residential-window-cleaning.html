What residential window cleaning actually is.

A professional residential window cleaning is the complete restoration of every glass surface on your home — interior and exterior — along with the frames, sills, and removable screens that surround them. It's not a quick wipe-down. A Trip's Windows service is a crew of trained, uniformed, background-checked technicians working through your home pane by pane, using professional-grade squeegees, deionized water, microfiber detailing cloths, and pH-balanced cleaners that are safe for tinted glass, window seals, pets, children, and plants.

Most Westside homeowners schedule a cleaning twice a year — once in spring after winter rain and pollen, and once in fall before holiday entertaining. Homes within a mile of the ocean (Venice, Santa Monica, Pacific Palisades, Manhattan Beach) typically need quarterly service because marine salt builds up on glass faster than inland dust.

Safe & streak-free

The products we use (and the ones we don't).

Most residential window cleaning is done with soap, water, and a squeegee — and that's exactly what we use on the majority of our jobs. For final rinses on high-visibility glass, we use deionized water, which dries spot-free because the mineral content has been stripped.

Our standard cleaning solution is pH-balanced and non-toxic. It's safe around pets, children, plants, tinted glass, and window seals. We avoid ammonia-based cleaners entirely — they can damage window films and degrade rubber seals over time.

For tougher jobs (hard water stains, post-construction residue, salt spray etching) we use specialty acidic cleaners and polishing compounds. These are applied surgically, only where needed, with proper containment.

The questions we hear most often. For the full list of 28 questions, see our complete FAQ.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

You don't have to be. Many of our customers leave a key, gate code, or arrange access through a property manager. Our team is uniformed and background-checked, and we're fully licensed and insured. If you'd prefer to be home, that's perfectly fine too.

How far in advance should I book?

Most residential jobs can be scheduled within 3 to 7 business days. Same-week and next-day service is often available — especially for smaller jobs and recurring customers. For peak periods (April–May, September–October, and the weeks before major holidays), we recommend booking 2 weeks ahead.

Are your products safe for my pets, kids, and plants?

Yes. We use pH-balanced, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe around pets, children, and plants. We avoid ammonia-based cleaners entirely because they can damage tinted glass and window seals.

What about second- and third-story windows?

Included. We routinely service two- and three-story residential homes using extension ladders, water-fed pole systems, and proper safety equipment. There's no upcharge for reasonable height access.

What's your guarantee?

Our 48-hour re-clean guarantee: if you're ever dissatisfied with any area of the service, let us know within 48 hours and we'll come back and re-clean at no additional charge. No questions, no haggling, no fees.
