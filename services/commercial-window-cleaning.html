What commercial window cleaning actually is.

Commercial window cleaning is the ongoing maintenance of every glass surface your customers and employees see — storefront windows, entry doors, interior glass partitions, display cases, and signage glass. Unlike a one-time residential cleaning, commercial service is built around recurring schedules that keep your business looking polished week after week, without you having to think about it.

A Trip's Windows commercial cleaning is performed by uniformed, background-checked technicians using professional-grade squeegees, deionized water rinse systems, and non-toxic cleaners that are safe for window tint, vinyl graphics, and painted frames. We work around your business hours — before open, after close, or during slow periods — so your customers never see a bucket or a ladder.

We currently service brands like Chipotle, Lululemon, Aesop, Starbucks, CVS, UNTUCKit, Purple, Van Leeuwen, and West Marine across Los Angeles, coordinating multi-location routes so every storefront stays consistent.

Frequency guide

How often does your business need window cleaning?

Depends on your foot traffic, location, and the impression you need to make.

High-traffic retail & restaurants
Weekly

Storefronts on busy streets, restaurants with large front windows, and any business where clean glass is part of the brand experience. Fingerprints, street dust, and weather spotting accumulate fast in high-traffic areas.

Standard retail & offices
Biweekly

Boutiques, professional offices, medical practices, and mid-traffic locations. Biweekly keeps things sharp without over-servicing. A strong choice for businesses off the main corridor.

Low-traffic & interior-only
Monthly

Office buildings with minimal street exposure, interior-only spaces, and businesses in low-dust environments. Monthly is the minimum we recommend for any commercial space open to the public.

Common questions

Commercial window cleaning — top questions.

The questions we hear most from business owners and property managers. For the full list, see our complete FAQ.

How does recurring billing work?

We invoice on a monthly cycle regardless of your service frequency. Weekly clients receive 4 or 5 visits per month at a flat monthly rate. Biweekly and monthly clients are billed per visit. Payment is by check, ACH, or credit card — whatever works best for your accounts payable process.

Can you clean before or after business hours?

Yes. Most of our commercial clients prefer early-morning (before 8 AM) or evening (after close) service. We're flexible and will work within whatever hours minimize disruption to your customers and staff. Our scheduling window runs from 6 AM to 10 PM.

Do you handle multi-location accounts?

Absolutely. We currently manage multi-location routes for several national and regional brands across LA. One point of contact, one invoice, consistent quality at every site. If you manage 2 locations or 20, the experience is the same.

What if it rains on our scheduled day?

If it's actively raining during your service window, we'll reschedule within 24 hours at no extra charge. Light rain or overcast days are no issue — glass actually cleans better when it's not baking in direct sunlight. We'll always communicate proactively about any weather delays.

Are you licensed and insured for commercial work?

Yes. We carry general liability insurance and can provide a certificate of insurance (COI) for your records or your landlord's requirements. All technicians are background-checked and uniformed.
