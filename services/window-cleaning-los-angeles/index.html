What's included

Every window cleaning includes the full treatment.

No cutting corners. No surprise charges. Just clean glass, every time.

Exterior glass

Professional squeegee cleaning of all exterior-facing glass using purified water. We remove dirt, grime, hard water spots, salt spray, and environmental buildup that accumulates on LA windows.

Interior glass

Interior side of every window cleaned and polished. We use drop cloths to protect your floors and furniture, and we're careful around blinds, curtains, and window treatments.

Frames & sills

Window frames and sills wiped down to remove dust, cobwebs, and debris. This is the detail that separates a professional job from a quick wipe — and it makes a noticeable difference.

Screens & tracks

Screens dusted and tracks vacuumed out. LA's dry climate fills tracks with dust and debris quickly — clean tracks mean windows that slide smoothly and seal properly.

Hard water removal

Mineral deposits from sprinklers and hard water leave white, chalky stains on glass that regular cleaning can't remove. We offer specialized hard water stain removal as an add-on service.

Skylights

Skylights are often the dirtiest windows in the house because they're out of sight and catch everything — dust, pollen, bird droppings, leaf debris. We clean them safely with the right equipment.

Frequently asked questions

Window cleaning in Los Angeles — common questions.

How much does window cleaning cost in Los Angeles?

Window cleaning costs in LA vary based on the number of windows, accessibility, and whether you need interior and exterior service. Most single-family homes range from $150 to $400 for a full cleaning. Multi-story homes and properties with skylights or hard-to-reach glass may be higher. We provide free, flat-rate estimates with no hidden fees — request a quote or call (310) 363-0781.

How often should I have my windows cleaned in LA?

For most LA homes, twice a year is the minimum — spring and fall. Coastal homes in Venice, Santa Monica, and the South Bay should clean quarterly because salt spray accelerates buildup. Commercial storefronts on busy corridors need weekly or biweekly cleaning. Properties near construction or heavy traffic may need more frequent service.

Do you clean both residential and commercial windows?

Yes. We provide both residential and commercial window cleaning across Los Angeles. Our commercial clients include national brands like Chipotle, Starbucks, and Lululemon, as well as independent businesses across LA's retail corridors.
