Screens that actually work.

A torn screen might not seem urgent until you're swatting flies out of the kitchen at dinner. Screens keep bugs out, let fresh air in, and protect your windows from debris. When they're damaged or missing, your home is less comfortable and less protected.

What we fix

Every type of screen, handled.

Residential or commercial, standard or custom.

Re-meshing

Torn or stretched mesh replaced with fresh fiberglass or aluminum screen material. Frame stays, mesh gets swapped.

Re-fitting

Screens that have popped out of their tracks or don't sit flush. We adjust, re-tension, and re-seat them so they stay put.

New screens

Missing a screen entirely? We measure, cut, and build new screens on-site to fit your exact window or door frame.

Sliding door screens

Patio and sliding door screens repaired or replaced. Rollers, tracks, and mesh all addressed so the door glides and closes properly.
