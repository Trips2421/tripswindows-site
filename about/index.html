About Us

We're not just in the neighborhood — we're part of it.

Trip's Windows is a family-run business built on meticulous work, honest service, and showing up like we said we would. Based in Venice, California since 2020.

How it started.

Trip's Windows was founded in 2020 in Venice, California — right in the middle of a time when most people were pulling back. We saw it differently. Neighbors needed help, homes needed care, and we knew we could do the work better than anyone.

What started as one guy with a squeegee and a ladder has grown into a full-service crew covering the Westside, the South Bay, the Valley, and beyond. We've cleaned everything from beachfront bungalows on the Venice Canals to commercial storefronts on Montana Avenue — and we bring the same standard to every job.

Today, Trip's Windows offers residential and commercial window cleaning, pressure washing, gutter cleaning, and screen repair across greater Los Angeles. We're still family-run, still based in Venice, and still obsessed with doing the job right.

What we stand for

Our approach to every job.

Meticulous work

We don't rush through jobs. Every window, every gutter, every square foot of hardscape gets the attention it deserves. If it's not right, we make it right — that's what the 48-hour re-clean guarantee is for.

Respectful service

We treat your property like it's our own. That means showing up on time, communicating clearly, cleaning up after ourselves, and never leaving you guessing about what's happening or what it costs.

Follow-through

We do what we say we're going to do. Flat-rate quotes with no surprises, appointments that hold, and work that speaks for itself. If something's not up to standard, call us — we'll be back within 48 hours.

Community-rooted

We live where we work. Venice is home, and the neighborhoods we serve are the same ones where our crew grabs coffee, walks their dogs, and raises their families. That matters to us.

Reliable scheduling

We know your time matters. That's why we give clear arrival windows, confirm appointments in advance, and keep you in the loop if anything changes. No ghosting, no "we'll be there sometime between 8 and 5."

Transparent pricing

Every quote is flat-rate — no hourly surprises, no hidden fees. We scope the job, give you an honest number, and that's what you pay. Simple as that.
