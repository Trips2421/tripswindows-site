Frequently asked questions

How often should I have my windows cleaned?

Most Los Angeles homeowners benefit from professional window cleaning twice a year — once in spring and once in fall. Homes near the coast (Venice, Santa Monica, Manhattan Beach) may need quarterly service because ocean salt builds up faster on glass.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Venice, Santa Monica, West LA, Brentwood, Pacific Palisades, Beverly Hills, Culver City, Manhattan Beach, and neighboring Los Angeles and Orange County communities.

Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes. Our Trip's promise is to make things right, every time. If you are ever dissatisfied with an area of service, let us know within 48 hours and we will re-clean your space at no additional charge.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Trip's Window Services is fully licensed and insured, with family-run ownership based in Venice, California since 2020.
