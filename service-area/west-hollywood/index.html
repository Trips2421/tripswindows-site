The West Hollywood factor

Why West Hollywood windows need regular professional cleaning.

West Hollywood's dense urban environment concentrates particulate in ways that suburban areas don't. High foot traffic on sidewalks kicks up dust and debris. Restaurants and bars exhaust grease particles into the air. The Sunset Strip's nightlife activity creates a constant background haze of urban pollution.

Construction is relentless in West Hollywood — new developments, renovations, building facade work, street repairs. This generates fine dust that settles on windows faster than in other neighborhoods. Even properties not under construction feel the effects.

Most importantly: West Hollywood is a design-conscious city. Dirty windows are immediately noticeable here. A film of dust that might be tolerable elsewhere becomes a statement about your property's care level. That's why the majority of our West Hollywood clients are on biweekly to monthly schedules — and the results speak for themselves.

What we recommend
  • Sunset Strip storefronts — weekly exterior glass. Presentation is everything on the Strip, and we deliver early morning before evening foot traffic.
  • Melrose Avenue businesses — weekly to biweekly. Curb appeal is currency in boutique retail. Keep storefronts immaculate.
  • Residential condos & apartments — biweekly to monthly. Building exteriors and unit windows in dense neighborhoods need regular attention.
  • High-rise and multi-story buildings — biweekly for exteriors. West Hollywood has significant vertical density — keep common areas pristine.
  • Pressure washing — quarterly for patios, terraces, and building facades. Urban grit accumulates fast in dense environments.
  • Gutter cleaning — twice a year. Palm fronds, construction debris, and urban dust clog gutters throughout the city.
