Common questions

Frequently asked questions about our service area.

What areas does Trip's Windows serve? +

Trip's Windows serves 29 neighborhoods across Los Angeles and Orange County, including Venice, Santa Monica, Beverly Hills, Pacific Palisades, Brentwood, West Hollywood, Bel Air, West LA, Culver City, Marina del Rey, Playa Vista, Playa del Rey, Mar Vista, Encino, Sherman Oaks, Studio City, Toluca Lake, Tarzana, Woodland Hills, Calabasas, Burbank, Atwater Village, Glendale, Pasadena, Manhattan Beach, El Segundo, Hermosa Beach, Redondo Beach, and select areas of Orange County.

Does Trip's Windows serve the San Fernando Valley? +

Yes. Trip's Windows runs dedicated Valley routes every week, covering Encino, Sherman Oaks, Studio City, Toluca Lake, Tarzana, Woodland Hills, and Calabasas. All five services — residential and commercial window cleaning, pressure washing, commercial pressure washing, and gutter cleaning — are available with no location surcharge.

Does Trip's Windows serve Pasadena and Glendale? +

Yes. Pasadena, Glendale, Burbank, Toluca Lake, and Atwater Village are all part of our northeast corridor routes. We run dedicated trucks to these areas weekly and offer the same services and pricing as our Westside routes.

Is there an extra charge for locations far from Venice? +

No. Trip's Windows charges the same rates across all 29 service areas. Whether you're in Venice, Calabasas, Pasadena, or Manhattan Beach, there are no travel fees, fuel surcharges, or distance-based upcharges.

How quickly can Trip's Windows schedule a job in my area? +

Most Westside neighborhoods (Venice, Santa Monica, West LA, Brentwood, Beverly Hills) can be scheduled within the same week. Valley, northeast, and South Bay areas typically book within one to two weeks depending on seasonal demand. Contact us for exact availability.

Does Trip's Windows serve Orange County? +

Yes, on a selective basis. We take jobs in parts of Orange County that align with our existing routes. Contact us with your address and we'll let you know if we can accommodate your location.

What services does Trip's Windows offer? +

Trip's Windows offers five core services: residential window cleaning, commercial window cleaning, residential pressure washing, commercial pressure washing, and gutter cleaning. All five services are available in every neighborhood we serve, with no minimum job size for residential work.
