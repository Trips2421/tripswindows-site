How far we travel

Your service area questions, answered.

Our home base is Venice, California — right on the Westside. From there, we cover a service area that stretches from Pacific Palisades in the north to Redondo Beach in the south, and inland through Brentwood, Beverly Hills, Culver City, and West LA.

For the South Bay (Manhattan Beach, Hermosa Beach, Redondo Beach, El Segundo), we run dedicated routes so scheduling is just as convenient as it is for our Westside clients.

For Orange County, we take jobs on a case-by-case basis — typically for larger residential properties, recurring commercial accounts, or multi-location businesses that extend south. If you're in OC, just ask — we'll let you know if we can reach you.

  • Venice, Santa Monica, West LA — same-week scheduling almost always available.
  • Brentwood, Palisades, Beverly Hills — core area, no travel surcharge.
  • Culver City, Mar Vista, Playa Vista — we're here daily.
  • Marina del Rey, Playa del Rey — adjacent to home base.
  • Manhattan Beach, Hermosa, Redondo, El Segundo — South Bay routes weekly.
  • Orange County — selective coverage, contact us to confirm.
