The Manhattan Beach factor

Why Manhattan Beach homes need regular professional cleaning.

Manhattan Beach sits directly on the Pacific, facing southwest — the direction that catches the full force of prevailing ocean winds, salt spray, and marine layer moisture. It's one of the most aggressive coastal environments for glass and exterior surfaces in all of Southern California.

The salt cycle is relentless. Ocean spray carries salt particles that settle on glass within hours of a cleaning. Morning marine layer adds moisture that picks up those particles, and when the fog burns off, it leaves behind a cloudy mineral film that builds layer by layer. South- and west-facing windows take the worst of it — but even inland-facing glass on a Strand home gets coated.

Manhattan Beach also has more glass per home than almost any other beach town. The new builds in the Sand Section and Hill Section are designed around views — floor-to-ceiling windows, multi-story glass walls, rooftop decks with glass railings. That's a lot of surface area exposed to the elements. Regular professional cleaning isn't optional here — it's essential to protecting your investment and enjoying the views you paid for.

What we recommend
  • Strand & Sand Section homes — monthly window cleaning. Direct oceanfront exposure means salt buildup is constant and heavy. Monthly is the standard for most Strand clients.
  • Hill Section homes — monthly or quarterly. Elevated but still coastal — wind-driven salt and dust settle on glass fast. Ocean-facing windows may need monthly attention.
  • Tree Section & East MB — quarterly window cleaning. Slightly less direct salt exposure, but still coastal. Tree Section homes also deal with pollen and sap.
  • Downtown businesses — weekly or biweekly exterior glass. Manhattan Beach's dining and retail scene demands pristine storefronts.
  • Pressure washing — annually for driveways, patios, pool decks, and walkways. Coastal moisture promotes algae, mildew, and salt staining on hardscape.
  • Gutter cleaning — once or twice a year. Tree Section and Hill Section homes with canopy coverage need biannual cleaning to prevent clogs.

How often should I clean my windows in Manhattan Beach?

Do you clean commercial storefronts in downtown Manhattan Beach?

Yes. We clean storefronts and restaurant glass along Manhattan Beach Boulevard, Highland Avenue, and the surrounding downtown blocks. Most of our Manhattan Beach commercial clients are on weekly or biweekly schedules, with early morning service before business hours.
