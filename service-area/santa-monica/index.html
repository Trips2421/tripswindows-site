The Santa Monica factor

Why Santa Monica homes need regular professional cleaning.

Santa Monica sits right on the coast, which means every home and business faces the same environmental reality as Venice: salt spray, marine layer moisture, and ocean-driven air that deposits mineral film on glass faster than most people expect.

The morning marine layer is the quiet culprit. It rolls in almost every night from May through September — sometimes year-round — and leaves a fine layer of moisture on every exterior surface. That moisture picks up airborne salt, dust, and pollution, and when it evaporates, it leaves behind a cloudy residue that builds up over weeks. South- and west-facing windows take the worst of it.

Add seasonal pollen from Santa Monica's abundant street trees, construction dust from the city's constant development, and brake dust from busy corridors like Wilshire and Lincoln, and the case for regular professional cleaning becomes clear. Most of our Santa Monica clients see a dramatic difference after their first cleaning — and stay on a recurring schedule after that.

What we recommend
  • Oceanfront homes & condos — monthly or bimonthly window cleaning. Direct salt spray exposure means faster buildup than anywhere else in the city.
  • North of Montana & inland homes — quarterly window cleaning. Still coastal, but slightly less direct exposure. Quarterly keeps glass clear year-round.
  • Montana Ave & Main St businesses — weekly or biweekly exterior glass. Storefronts need to look perfect for the foot traffic these corridors attract.
  • Pressure washing — annually for driveways, patios, pool decks, and walkways. Coastal moisture promotes algae and mildew on hardscape.
  • Gutter cleaning — twice a year if you have mature trees, once a year for most Santa Monica homes. Prevents overflow and foundation water damage.
  • Screen repair — as needed. Salt air corrodes screen frames and mesh faster than inland locations.
