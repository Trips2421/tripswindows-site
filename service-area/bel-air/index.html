The Bel Air factor

Why Bel Air estates need professional monthly cleaning.

Bel Air's geography and character create conditions unlike any other LA neighborhood. The canyons and elevated terrain concentrate dust, pollen, and debris naturally. The mature landscaping — towering sycamores, coastal live oaks, Monterey pines — constantly sheds sap, leaves, and flower particles onto windows and gutters.

Construction is perpetual. Whether it's on your property or a neighbor's, renovation dust settles on glass quickly. Many Bel Air estates are perpetually under some form of remodel or upgrade, meaning windows accumulate fine particulate matter faster than in less active neighborhoods.

Then there's the standard itself. When your home commands multi-million dollar valuations and every window frames a panoramic view, clarity matters. Dirty windows compromise the entire purpose of having floor-to-ceiling glass. That's why our Bel Air clients understand monthly service isn't an option — it's essential.

What we recommend
  • All Bel Air estates — monthly window cleaning. The combination of mature landscaping, canyon dust, and sap means windows require regular attention. Monthly is the standard for showroom appearance.
  • Upper Bel Air & Stone Canyon homes — monthly, or consider bi-weekly during spring pollen season. Elevated exposure and canyon effects are most pronounced here.
  • Multi-story estates — plan for 2 to 3-day deep cleaning jobs. We scale crews to your property size and schedule jobs strategically to minimize disruption.
  • Pressure washing — annually for motor courts, patios, and pool decks. Bel Air hardscape accumulates sap stains and canyon dust steadily.
  • Gutter cleaning — twice a year minimum, quarterly in spring. Mature tree debris clogs gutters rapidly in Bel Air's landscape.
  • Ongoing property monitoring — let us know about construction activity in your area. We can schedule more frequent service if needed during active renovation periods.
