The Venice factor

Why Venice homes need more frequent cleaning.

Living near the ocean is beautiful — but it's hard on glass. Venice sits directly on the Pacific, which means every home and business deals with a unique combination of environmental factors that mainland LA neighborhoods don't face.

Salt spray is the biggest culprit. Airborne salt particles settle on glass within hours of a cleaning and gradually build into a hazy, cloudy film that dulls natural light and obscures views. Add morning marine layer moisture, seasonal pollen, and fine construction dust from Venice's constant building activity, and windows deteriorate fast.

That's why most of our Venice clients are on a quarterly schedule — and oceanfront properties often go monthly. It's the difference between glass that looks clean and glass that actually lets the light in.

What we recommend
  • Oceanfront / canal-front homes — monthly or quarterly window cleaning. Salt exposure is constant and heavy.
  • Walk streets & interior Venice — quarterly window cleaning. Still coastal, but slightly less direct salt exposure.
  • Abbot Kinney & Rose Ave businesses — weekly or biweekly exterior glass. Storefronts need to look perfect for foot traffic.
  • Pressure washing — annually for driveways, patios, and walkways. Venice's sand and grime accumulate fast.
  • Gutter cleaning — twice a year if you have tree coverage, once a year for most Venice homes. Prevents overflow and water damage.
  • Screen repair — as needed. Salt air corrodes screen frames faster than inland locations.

Venice FAQ

Questions about service in Venice.

How quickly can you schedule a cleaning in Venice?

Since Venice is our home base, we can almost always get you on the schedule within the same week. For recurring clients, we lock in a standing time slot so you never have to think about it.

How often should I clean my windows in Venice?

We recommend quarterly for most Venice homes. Oceanfront and canal-front properties often go monthly because salt spray accumulates so quickly. Interior Venice homes (between Lincoln and the canals) can sometimes stretch to every 4–6 months, but quarterly gives the best results year-round.

Can you access homes on Venice walk streets and canals?

Yes — we're experienced with Venice's unique access situations. We carry equipment by hand on walk streets, use water-fed poles for canal-side glass that's hard to reach from the ground, and know how to navigate shared driveways, locked gates, and narrow footpaths throughout the neighborhood.

Do you clean storefronts on Abbot Kinney and Rose Avenue?

Yes. We clean multiple storefronts on both Abbot Kinney and Rose Avenue on recurring weekly and biweekly schedules. We typically arrive early in the morning before businesses open so there's no disruption to foot traffic or operations.

Is there a minimum job size for Venice?

No. Since we're based in Venice, there's no minimum and no travel surcharge. Whether it's a small studio apartment or a large canal-front home, we'll take care of it.
