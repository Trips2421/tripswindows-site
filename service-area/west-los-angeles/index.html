The West LA factor

Why West LA homes need regular professional cleaning.

West Los Angeles sits at a unique environmental crossroads — the 405/10 interchange to the south and west, Sawtelle's construction and development activity, and enough marine influence to bring salt spray from the coast. The result: a perfect storm of window-dirtying factors that hit West LA harder than many other Westside neighborhoods.

The 405/10 freeway junction is the main culprit. Millions of vehicles a month pass through those interchanges, kicking up brake dust, tire particulate, and diesel emissions. That grime travels north and east on the wind, settling on every south- and west-facing surface in West LA. Factor in the constant construction — new buildings going up around Sawtelle, apartment renovations throughout the neighborhood, and utility work — and you add construction dust and debris to the mix.

Add urban pollution, seasonal pollen from West LA's abundant street trees, and occasional salt drift from the coast, and the case for regular professional cleaning becomes clear. Most of our West LA clients see a dramatic difference after their first cleaning — and stay on a quarterly schedule after that.

What we recommend
  • Near 405/10 interchange — quarterly to semi-annual window cleaning. Freeway proximity means heavier particulate accumulation than inland West LA.
  • Sawtelle & high-traffic areas — quarterly window cleaning. Construction dust and development activity mean windows accumulate grime faster than quiet residential streets.
  • Rancho Park & quieter streets — semi-annual to quarterly. Tree coverage and lower traffic means slower accumulation, but quarterly keeps glass clear year-round.
  • Pressure washing — annually for driveways, patios, and walkways. Urban grime and pollen promote algae and mildew on hardscape. More frequent in high-pollen seasons.
  • Gutter cleaning — twice a year if you have mature trees (especially Rancho Park), once a year for most West LA homes. Prevents overflow and foundation water damage.
  • Screen repair — as needed. Urban environment and constant development mean screens get torn and corroded faster than suburban areas.
